A native Android app for snoozing (silencing calls and notifications via DND) for minutes to hours.

Suzy Snooze enables Do Not Disturb mode, sets ring and notification volumes to 0, and switches ringer mode to Silent (no vibrate). Alarms and media playback remain fully functional - volumes are never touched. After the snooze period, the app restores normal ringer mode, turns off DND, and restores original volumes (minimum 3 to ensure audibility).

Key Features:
- DND Priority mode with explicit policy allowing alarms and media
- Mutes only ring + notification streams (STREAM_RING, STREAM_NOTIFICATION)
- Never touches alarm or media volumes (STREAM_ALARM, STREAM_MUSIC preserved)
- Auto-restore with sound notification when snooze completes
- Works on Android 10+ (API 29+)
